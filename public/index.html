kindlebility

Sending articles to your Kindle, one click at a time! Kindlebility gives you a bookmarklet that sends (almost) any article to your Kindle for easy reading at a later time. In one click. Enter your Kindle email (@kindle.com or @free.kindle.com), drag the bookmarklet to your bookmark bar, visit an article, and hit "Send to my Kindle!". In a few minutes you should have the article on your Kindle so you can read it later. I don't keep anything, and you can check out the source here.

Drag the link below to your bookmarks bar, and add kindle@darkhelmetlive.com to your Kindle Approved E-mail List
